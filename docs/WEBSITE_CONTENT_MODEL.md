## Website Type
Phase 1 should be a structured one-page project website for the Dorostol Trade oil and vegetable-protein plant project.

It must not be a weak one-page brochure. Each section needs a distinct purpose, clear entity signals, meaningful headings, and strong navigation anchors.

It must not present Dorostol Trade as an engineering-services company or as a general trading and transport platform.

Future multi-page expansion is possible only after more verified content, technical detail, approved claims, and runtime assets become available.

## Primary Goal
Present the plant project credibly and clearly to relevant commercial, institutional, investor, and partner audiences, then route qualified interest to Dorostol Trade.

## Target Audiences
- Commercial partners.
- Investors and financing stakeholders.
- Buyers or customers for plant proteins, protein isolate, vegetable oils, or related outputs.
- Raw-material suppliers and farming communities.
- Relevant Romanian and EU institutions.
- Strategic partners.

Audience priority order requires management approval.

## Conversion Goals
Primary conversion:
- Contact Dorostol Trade.

Secondary conversion:
- Discuss partnership opportunities.

Supporting outcomes:
- Understand the project scope.
- Understand products and raw materials.
- Understand plant location and reported advantages.
- Understand Year 5 production targets.
- Establish credibility for follow-up discussions.

## Section-by-Section Content Model
| Section | Purpose | Core content | CTA or outcome |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Hero | Identify the project immediately. | Approved project title, concise project-focused value proposition, soybean/sunflower/rapeseed context, Andrasesti location. | `Contact Dorostol Trade` |
| Project Overview | Establish credibility and scope. | Dorostol Trade establishment in Romania in 2013, plant-project summary, April 2025 investment agreement, reported budget. | Continue to project details. |
| Market Opportunity | Explain why the project matters without overstating claims. | Approved vegetable-protein and oil market context, regional agricultural context, local raw-material relevance. | Understand business relevance. |
| Plant Location | Explain geographic advantages. | Andrasesti, Romania; proximity to agricultural zones; access to Constanta port; EU market access; local raw-material availability. | Understand logistics context. |
| Products | Describe intended outputs. | Plant proteins, protein isolate, vegetable oils, and approved active-molecule references where appropriate. | Identify commercial relevance. |
| Capacity & Operations | Present targets accurately. | Year 5 target: 35,500 MT final product annually, including 27,500 MT plant protein and 8,000 MT vegetable oils. Clearly label figures as targets, not current results. | Understand planned scale. |
| Technology & Processing Focus | Explain the plant at an approved high level. | Extraction and processing focus; soybean, sunflower, and rapeseed inputs. Add process detail only after technical review. | Understand the project concept. |
| Sustainability & Impact | Present approved impact themes. | Reduced-carbon theme compared with animal-protein production, agricultural by-product use, support for European and local farming communities. Avoid quantitative claims until verified. | Understand impact themes. |
| Investor / Partner CTA | Route qualified interest. | Concise approved invitation for commercial, investor, and strategic-partner discussions. | `Discuss Partnership Opportunities` |
| Contact | Provide approved contact routes. | Website, general email, Radostin Atanasov, direct email, Romania phone, Bulgaria phone after final approval. | `Contact Dorostol Trade` |

## SEO/GEO Entity Targets
Use clear, factual language that helps search engines and AI answer engines identify:
- Dorostol Trade SRL / LTD, pending final legal-name approval.
- Dorostol Trade oil and vegetable-protein plant project.
- Extraction and processing plant.
- Plant proteins.
- Protein isolate.
- Vegetable oils.
- Soybean.
- Sunflower.
- Rapeseed.
- Andrasesti, Romania.
- Constanta port.
- Romania and EU market context.

Use meaningful section headings, consistent entity wording, descriptive anchor links, and internal section linking. Do not add unverified schema, metadata claims, or market-superiority language.

## Claims Allowed in Public Copy
Use only after final management approval of wording:
- Dorostol Trade was established in Romania in 2013.
- The project focuses on plant proteins, protein isolate, and oils from soybean, sunflower, and rapeseed.
- The plant location is Andrasesti, Romania.
- The project has an investment agreement signed in April 2025 with the Romanian government.
- The total project budget is EUR 43,914,517.53.
- Year 5 target production is 35,500 MT final product annually, including 27,500 MT plant protein and 8,000 MT vegetable oils.
- The location provides proximity to agricultural zones, access to Constanta port, EU market access, and local raw-material availability.

Public copy must distinguish project plans and targets from current operating results.

## Claims Requiring Verification
- Profit-margin projections.
- EU market-share capture.
- Leadership claims.
- Global and EU forecast numbers.
- Sustainability claims if expressed quantitatively.
- Technical process details not described in the PDF.
- Non-GMO or clean-label claims unless separately verified.
- Active-molecule wording, product grades, specifications, and applications.
- Government-agreement attribution wording.
- Financial projections, return claims, and investment-performance claims.

## Deferred Content
- Public downloadable PDF or project brief.
- Detailed process diagrams.
- Detailed product specification pages.
- Multi-page expansion.
- News, blog, or insights content.
- Unsupported partner logos, visual assets, or case studies.
- Engineering-services, broad-trade, or general-transport positioning.
