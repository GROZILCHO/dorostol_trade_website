Project-focused structure proposed for management confirmation. Final format and route paths remain subject to explicit approval.

| Page | Purpose |
| --- | --- |

| Project / The Plant | Present the plant concept, scope, and approved business value. |
| Products | Present approved vegetable-protein, protein-isolate, and oil products. |

## Deferred Sections
Do not implement engineering-services, broad trade, general transport, generic corporate-service, blog, or unsupported capability sections unless a later documented management decision explicitly brings them into scope.
